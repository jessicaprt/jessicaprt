Starting with some random facts about me, I grew up in the Philippines where I lived for 18 years before moving to Calgary, Alberta. I am a music-lover! My free time involves playing my guitar or my ukulele and singing. I love going on adventures and taking pictures of everything. Photography is definitely one of my favourites.

I love working with computers to state the obvious (given my chosen undergraduate deree). Working in the tech industry is definitely what I want to do with my life. I am really interested on web and app development cause it's where I really get to apply my creativity. I know there are a lot of other stuff out there that I have yet to discover. And as I do so, my passion for tech just grows!

I love being involved with my community. Within the university, especially within the faculty of Engineering, I have joined various student groups and worked with my fellow students.
