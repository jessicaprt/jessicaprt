About Me

I am a Computer Engineering undergraduate from the University of Alberta.

Starting with some random facts about me,
I grew up in the Philippines where I lived for 18 years before moving to Canada. I enjoy playing my guitar and ukulele and I also enjoy singing. Photography is one of my favourite things to do. I love going to different places and just take pictures (you can look at some of my photos at the Gallery section).

I love working with computers! If that's not yet obvious basen on my chosen undergraduate degree.
My interest is mostly on web and app development. When I started learning how to write code, I could not stop wanting to learn more. As I learn more skills, I grow more passionate and more motivated to keep developing my knowledge.

I love being involved with my community. Within the university, especially within the faculty of Engineering, I have joined various student groups and worked with my fellow students.

  • Students' Union - Orientation Leader (2014; 2015)
  • Engineering Students' Society - Memories Coordinator (2014-2015; 2015-2016)
  • Engineering Students' Society - Pi Throw Coordinator (2015-2016)
  • Computer Engineering Club - Vice-President Social (2015-2016)
  • Computer Engineering Club - President (2016-2017)

I love getting more experiences by joining conferences and competitions.

  • competed in HackEd 2016: Computer Engineering Club's Annual 24-hour hackathon
  • attended the Student Developer Conference by Startup Edmonton

Projects

Here I have some of my projects that I have worked with during my university classes, as well as some personal projects that I have done.

Tic Tac Toe

This impossible game of tic tac toe was implemented by using the minimax algorithm in order to build an AI such that the computer will always win, or the game results in a draw. [Python]

Snake - on Arduino

For the hardware components of this version of the classic snake game, a joystick and an LCD (Adafruit) was connected to an Arduino Mega. This game was written in C with the use of the Arduino library.
