ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Jessica Prieto. I am a Computer Engineering undergraduate at the University of Alberta.

I enjoy my time learning more about the computer engineering world through my academics, learning by myself, or by being around people with a similar passion. I have met a lot of people with the same interests in technology by being involved with the Computer Engineering Club where I currently serve as the VP Social-- I organize academic and social events by the club and I take part in networking with other companies to get them involved.

I regularly engage in different activities in university. Aside from my involvement with the Computer Engineering club, I also have a coordinator position with the Engineering Students’ Society at the U of A.
